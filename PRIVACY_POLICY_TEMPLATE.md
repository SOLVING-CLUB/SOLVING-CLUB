# Privacy Policy Template for Google OAuth

You'll need a privacy policy URL for Google OAuth verification. Here's a template you can use:

---

## Privacy Policy for SOLVING-CLUB

**Last Updated: [Date]**

### Information We Collect

SOLVING-CLUB uses Google OAuth to access your Google Calendar for the following purposes:

1. **Calendar Access**: We access your Google Calendar to create calendar events with Google Meet links when you schedule meetings in our application.

2. **Meeting Links**: We generate Google Meet links and associate them with meetings you create in our project management system.

### How We Use Your Information

- **Calendar Events**: We create calendar events in your Google Calendar when you schedule meetings through our application. These events include meeting details, participants, and Google Meet links.

- **Data Storage**: Meeting information (including Google Meet links) is stored in our database to allow you and your team members to access meeting details.

- **No Data Sharing**: We do not share your calendar data with third parties. Your calendar information is only used to create and manage meetings within our application.

### Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data:
- OAuth tokens are stored securely in your browser's local storage
- All API communications use HTTPS encryption
- We follow Google's security best practices for OAuth implementation

### Your Rights

You can revoke Google Calendar access at any time by:
1. Going to [Google Account Settings](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions)
2. Finding "SOLVING-CLUB" in the list of connected apps
3. Clicking "Remove Access"

### Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: [Your Email]
